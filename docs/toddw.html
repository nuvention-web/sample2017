Todd's Background

Todd attended Northwestern in the 80's and then worked at Microsoft for more than 20 years on a variety of products, including development tools, Microsoft Exchange, Windows Server, Microsoft Small Business Server, Mirosoft Project, Live Maps / Virtual Earth, Windows Embedded and Windows Mobile

Since leaving Microsoft Todd has been teaching at Northwestern, and is also a partner in a Venture Capital firm in Seattle, Divergent Ventures
