NUvention Web 2017 Teaching Team

The NUvention Web Teaching team combines industry and academic experience

NUvention Web is a course offered jointly by the Farley Center in the McCormick School School of Engineering and the Medill School of Journalism. It's mission is to provide students with a curricular experience that simulates an internet or internet media startup over 20 weeks. Students work extensively to identify an opportunity, build a prototype, test a business model, and launch on the web; getting real customers and real feedback on their execution. We are well supported by our incredible set of teaching assistants that help make the course work
