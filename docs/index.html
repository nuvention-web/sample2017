NUvention Web 2017

NUvention Web is a multidisciplinary two quarter course where students build and launch internet businesses

NUvention Web is a course offered jointly by the Farley Center in the McCormick School School of Engineering and the Medill School of Journalism. It's mission is to provide students with a curricular experience that simulates an internet or internet media startup over 20 weeks. Students work extensively to identify an opportunity, build a prototype, test a business model, and launch on the web; getting real customers and real feedback on their execution. You can read more about our team and our incredible set of teaching assistants that help make the course work Our goals:

Identify a business opportunity

Build a product that meets user needs

Work in a multidisciplinary team
